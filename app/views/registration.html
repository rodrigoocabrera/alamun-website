Register Now!

Step 1

The first step of the registration process is the school registration. To do this you must be a school advisor. This step can be done by sending an email to registration@alamun.com. Please include the number of students that will attend the event.

Step 2

You will be sent a confirmation email that includes the committees & delegations information. Upon reception of this email, you will need to send us with your list of the students with their desire delegations. This list is subject to availability.

Step 3

You'll receive your final list along with the payment information. Please keep your receipt and either email it to us or bring it the day of the event.

Independent Delegates

If your school isn't attending you can register as an independent delegate, just email us at registration@alamun.com with your full name, age, grade, committee and delegation you wish to represent. You'll receive a confirmation email with the next steps to follow.
